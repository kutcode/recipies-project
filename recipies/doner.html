Doner Kebab Sandwich

A classic sandwich consisting of sliced ground lamb and spices/veggies.

One of my favorite Turkish street foods.
My go-to comfort dish whenever I am trying to choose something to pick up on the street in Turkey.

Ingredients: For the Kebab

  • 1 pound ground lamb (or 1/2 pound each of ground lamb and ground beef)
  • 1 large egg
  • 4 clove garlic, peeled and finely minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil, more for the pan

Ingredients: For the Sandwich

  • 4 large pita, naan, or flatbread
  • 1 cup assorted lettuce leaves
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1/2 medium English cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 large red onion, peeled and sliced
  • Tzatziki sauce or tahini sauce

Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Pre heat the oven to 350 F. In a large bowl, combine the ground lamb, egg, garlic, ground cumin, ground coriander, smoked paprika, dried
    oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place the mixture into an oiled 9 x 5-inch loaf pan and cook in the oven for approximately 30 minutes or until the top is a light golden brown.
  4. You can slice the loaf immediately if you like but, for best results, cool completely, wrap in aluminum foil, and refrigerate until firm.
  5. To reheat, add a little olive oil to a large skillet, slice the loaf very thinly and crisp up the slices in the hot pan for a few minutes.
  6. Assemble the sandwiches with pita, warmed and toasted through. Spread on some tzatziki or tahini sauce, add lettuce, tomato, cucumber, onion,
    and top with more sauce.
